
--------------------
Size Information:

Original Size: 16x16
VX/VXA: 32x32 (2x)
MV/MZ: 48x48 (3x)
--------------------
Instructions

1. Place the files of adequate size in the tileset graphics folder of your project.
2. Setup collisions and tilesheets to your liking. Select "Area Type" for the A2 sheets, for the skins to work correctly.
3. For the tiles in the "A5" row, to make usage of the transparent features of rocks and cliffs, put a duplicate of the set in the "C" tab of your tileset onwards.
